🚇 Sainðaul Metro — 19 lines · 673.93 km

The Sainðaul Metro (Capital Subway) is the largest urban rail network in Izaland and one of the most extensive in the Axian region, with 19 lines covering the metropolitan area of the capital city. Lines are integrated with Izarail suburban rail and the IZX Keishin Line at Sainðaul Central (K01).

🚇 Warohan Metro — 8 lines · 217.90 km

The Warohan Metro serves Izaland’s second city and the southern terminus of the IZX Keishin Line (K17) and the Nankai Line (K17–N4). The network interconnects with the IZX station at Warohan Central.

🚇 Panaireki Metro — 8 lines · 193.81 km

Panaireki’s metro network is one of the busiest outside the capital, serving the city’s commercial and industrial districts. The IZX Keishin station (K12) provides a direct intercity link.

🚇 Kichatsura Metro — 4 lines · 93.47 km

Kichatsura’s four metro lines radiate from the IZX Keishin hub at Shin-Kichatsura Teba (K10), where the Sakamuso Branch also converges.

🚇 Sannupuri Subway — 4 lines · 107.59 km
