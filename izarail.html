Izarail — Urban & Suburban Networks

Izarail

The national rail operator of Izaland, covering commuter, regional and intercity services. Izarail operates the Capital Suburban Network from Sainðaul Central and urban metro-style lines in major cities.

Capital Suburban Network
11 commuter lines radiating from Sainðaul Central, one of Izaland's busiest terminals. Connects the capital to the broader metropolitan region.
