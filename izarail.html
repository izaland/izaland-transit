Izarail — Urban & Suburban Networks

Urban Mass Transit
across Izaland

Izarail operates, or co-ordinates with private operators, the urban and suburban rail networks in Izaland's major cities. From the sprawling capital region of Sainðaul to the industrial and portual corridors of Warohan and the northern gateway of Sannupuri, every major conurbation is served by a dedicated local mass-transit network.
