IZX Keishin Line 京深線

The Keishin Line (京深線, Keishin-sen) is IZX's primary coastal corridor, running 569.6 km from Sainðaul Central to Warohan, where it continues onto the Nankai Line through to Daishin. Three distinct service patterns share the infrastructure: the coastal trunk (services A–D), the Airport Bypass via the inner corridor (K31–K33), and the mountain Sakamuso Branch (services E–F) which rejoins the main line at Shin-Kichatsura Teba.

A — Super Express (Nozomi-type, limited stops)
B — Express (Hikari-type)
C — Local / Airport Shuttle (two sub-services)
E — Sakamuso Branch Super Express
F — Sakamuso Branch Local
G — Nankai through service
